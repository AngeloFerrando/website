Small Intro : Angelo Ferrando is a Postdoctoral Research Associate at University of Liverpool. His research interests are focused on Multi-Agent Systems (MASs) and Runtime Verification (RV). Since from his master's thesis he has been working on the design and implementation of a compact and expressive formalism (based on Session Types theory) to be used for the RV of MASs: the Trace Expression formalism. With respect to the standard Centralized approach, he has been trying to follow a more decentralized way to solve this kind of problems, in particular studying the possible use of the Decentralized Runtime Verification (DRV) to improve the efficiency avoiding bottlenecks issues during the runtime monitoring of big MASs.
